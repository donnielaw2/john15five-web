Support & Help

Answers to common questions — and a real person to reach when you need one.

John15Five is a private, local-first Bible app. The Bible is on your device, everything you write stays encrypted on your phone, and there's no account to set up. Here's how to get the most from it.

Frequently asked questions

Is my information private?

Yes. Your journal, prayers, highlights, and notes are encrypted on your device and never leave it. There's no account, no ads, and no tracking of what you read. Full details are in our Privacy Policy.

Does it work without an internet connection?

The Bible and all reading, journaling, and prayer work fully offline. Only the optional study companion and AI plan features need a connection.

How do I start a reading plan?

Open Plans and choose one of three ways: pick a ready-made plan, build one for what you're walking through, or photograph a printed plan from church to import it.

How do I highlight a verse or add a note?

Press and hold a verse while reading. You can highlight it, add a note, mark it a favorite, or add it to a named group.

Can I speak instead of type?

Yes — tap the microphone in any writing box (journal, prayer, or note) to dictate. Nothing you say is saved as a recording; it's turned into text on your device.

How do I have a passage read aloud?

Tap Listen at the top of the reader. It reads the passage using your phone's built-in voice. You can adjust the speed and voice in Settings → Reading aloud.

How do I share a plan, or open one someone sent me?

Open a plan and tap Share this plan to send a copy (it carries the passages only — never your journal, prayers, or progress). To open a plan someone sent you, go to Plans → Open a plan file.

How do I delete a plan, mark, or entry?

Open the plan and use Delete this plan at the bottom of its screen. For marks and verse groups, press and hold the item to remove it.

I found a bug or have an idea. What do I do?

We'd love to hear it. Use Settings → Send feedback in the app, or email us directly below.
